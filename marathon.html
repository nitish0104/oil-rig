MARATHON

Marathon Heater India Private Limited is one of the largest heater manufacturing company in India. In 2007 with joint venture between Marathon Heater Inc. USA and Tempsens Instruments India started manufacturing heaters. Marathon Heaters are also a part of Pyrotech Group, established by four technocrats in 1976 at Udaipur, with Thermocouples and RTDs as their first product. The company has collaborated with world leaders in Thermal engineering technologies for non-contact temperature measurement, heaters, furnaces and other critical components.
